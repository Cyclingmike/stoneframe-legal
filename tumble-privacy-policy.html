Privacy Policy — Tumble

Last updated: July 2026

Tumble is developed by StoneFrame (Michael Holland, sole trader). This policy explains what data Tumble collects and how it's used.

Data we collect directly

Tumble does not require an account and does not collect or store any personal information on our own servers. Your score, level progress, and settings are saved locally on your device only, and are not transmitted to us.

Advertising

Tumble uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as your device's advertising identifier) to show ads, and — where you've given permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt — to personalise them. If you don't grant permission, you'll still see ads, but they won't be personalised to you.

Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can find more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

In-app purchases

Tumble offers an optional in-app purchase to remove ads. All purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store — StoneFrame never sees or stores your payment details, card information, or Apple ID billing data. We only store a simple local flag on your device recording whether ads have been removed. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, you can restore your purchase using the "Restore Purchases" option, which checks your purchase history with Apple directly.

Children's privacy

Tumble is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can reset the App Tracking Transparency permission or limit ad tracking at any time in your device's Settings app, under Privacy & Security. Since we don't hold any personal data on our servers, there's no account data for us to delete on request — but if you have any questions about your data, you can contact us using the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, if we add new features that use data differently. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@stoneframe.co.uk.
