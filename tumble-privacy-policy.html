Privacy Policy — Tumble

Last updated: July 2026

Tumble is developed by StoneFrame (Michael Holland, sole trader). This policy explains what data Tumble collects and how it's used.

Data we collect directly

Tumble does not require an account and does not collect or store any personal information on our own servers. Your score, level progress, settings, and free-play counts are saved locally on your device only, and are not transmitted to us.

Advertising and tracking

Tumble does not display advertising and does not use any third-party advertising or analytics services. No device identifiers or usage data are collected for advertising purposes, and Tumble does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

In-app purchases

Tumble offers 2 free plays each of Classic and Challenge mode. After these are used, players can make an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Unlock Tumble") for unlimited play in both modes. All purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store — StoneFrame never sees or stores your payment details, card information, or Apple ID billing data. We only store a simple local record on your device of your remaining free plays and whether Tumble has been unlocked. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, you can restore your purchase using the "Restore Purchases" option, which checks your purchase history with Apple directly.

Children's privacy

Tumble is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

Since we don't hold any personal data on our servers, there's no account data for us to delete on request — but if you have any questions about your data, you can contact us using the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, if we add new features that use data differently. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@stoneframe.co.uk.
