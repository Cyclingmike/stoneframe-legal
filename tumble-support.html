Frequently asked

My saved game disappeared.
Tumble saves your game locally on your device. If you've reinstalled the app, switched devices, or cleared your device storage, unfortunately saved games can't be recovered.

I bought Remove Ads but still see ads / I'm on a new device.
Open Settings from the main menu and tap "Restore Purchases" — this checks your purchase history with Apple and reapplies it.

How do leaderboards work?
Tumble uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device's Settings app.
